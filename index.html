Your Partners Have Quotas Too

Sellers, CS leaders, and execs at partner companies are measured on their numbers—not yours. Asking them to learn your product and carve out time for referrals means asking them to deprioritize what they're actually paid to do.

The Learning Curve Is a Non-Starter
