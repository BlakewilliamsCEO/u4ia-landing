Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Gmail?

Sign in at u4ia.ai and follow the onboarding flow to grant Gmail access. The Chrome extension does not access Gmail directly — all email analysis happens through the web app.

How are opportunities scored?

U4IA analyzes your conversations for ICP fit, intent signals, and buying readiness. Only the top ~1% of contacts that match a workspace owner's ideal customer profile are surfaced as opportunities.

How do payouts work?

Each opportunity has a milestone ladder with specific payout amounts. As you complete milestones (confirm intent, make introduction, meeting booked, meeting attended), payouts are approved and added to your balance for redemption.

How do I disconnect my data?

Go to your Account tab in the extension or visit u4ia.ai to manage connections. You can disconnect Gmail, Calendar, or delete your account at any time.

What data does the Chrome extension access?

The extension only uses Google Sign-In (email and profile) to identify your account. It does not read email, calendar, or any website content. All analysis happens server-side through the U4IA web app.
