The CAC Waste Problem

Warm closes. Cold scales.
Nothing does both.

Ads buy impressions, not intent. Outbound manufactures outreach, not timing. Referrals are powerful — but informal and unaccountable. Until now.

CAC keeps rising

Paid media gets noisier every quarter. You pay for impressions, clicks, and guesses — then hope it turns into pipeline.

Warm wins — but it's informal

Introductions close because they're trusted. But they live in conversation threads — not in a system you can scale.

Trust has no routing layer

You can't see who should connect you, when the timing is right, or what the next step should be. So warm revenue stays accidental.

Attribution is broken

Most referral programs pay on volume or vibes. Without milestone verification and enforcement, incentives invite spam and trust collapses.
