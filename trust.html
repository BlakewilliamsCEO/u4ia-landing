Security Controls

Active

All OAuth tokens are encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption before storage. Database contents are encrypted at rest via PostgreSQL's native encryption.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you read my internal company emails?
No. Internal emails are filtered out in your browser before they ever reach our servers. We only analyze conversations with external parties. This is enforced technically, not just by policy.
What email data do you store?
We store email snippets (not full bodies), extracted signals (like pain points and buying intent), and metadata (sender domain, subject line). Full email content is processed but not persisted.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. Email privacy@u4ia.ai with subject "Data Deletion Request" and we'll delete all your data within 30 days. You can also disconnect your Gmail/Calendar from the extension settings at any time.
Is my data used to train AI models?
No. We use Anthropic's Claude API which has a zero-retention policy for API customers. Your data is not used to train any AI models.
Who can see my opportunities?
Only you. Tenant administrators (like your partner program manager) can see aggregate metrics but cannot access individual email content or specific opportunity details.
Are you SOC 2 certified?
Not yet. We implement SOC 2-aligned controls (as documented on this page) but have not completed a formal audit. SOC 2 Type II certification is on our roadmap as we scale.
